Heya,
my name is Toa Heftiba.

I am an awarded Food & Product Photographer, Marketer and a Creative Designer - with a crush on Creativity and my Husband.

About me

Being born in Transylvania I did not have many creative opportunities but I did finish college as a ‘ Text and Image Process Operator ‘ and studied ‘Art and Design’ at my local UNI.

I have worked as a Graphic Designer for a good well-known TV studio, I have assisted well-known Photographers even worked as a freelancer for quite a while, but everywhere I worked or got involved I had to enter in this little tiny box. And when I’m being kept in a little box, I love to poke holes through which I let my creativity break out. This way, I start doing everything the way I would do it best.
